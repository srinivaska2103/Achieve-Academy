Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Can I switch plans after enrolling?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time. The remaining fees are adjusted proportionally. Downgrading is available at the end of the billing cycle. Contact our admissions desk for plan transitions.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

We offer a 7-day free trial for all plans. If you are not satisfied within the first 7 days, we provide a full refund, no questions asked. After 7 days, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships of up to 50% for students from economically weaker sections. Additionally, students who score 90%+ in our entrance assessment receive automatic 25% scholarship on any plan.
